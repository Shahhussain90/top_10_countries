Thailand

Thailand is often hailed as one of the most beautiful countries in the world, boasting stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine. One of the standout features of this Southeast Asian gem is its famous temples, which are not just places of worship but also architectural wonders.
One of the most iconic temples in Thailand is Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn. Situated on the banks of the Chao Phraya River in Bangkok, Wat Arun is a sight to behold with its intricate mosaic tiles, towering spires , and ornate carvings. The temple is named after the Hindu god Aruna, symbolizing the dawn and the rising sun.
